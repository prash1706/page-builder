What is the API economy?

Exposing digital services to employees, partners and consumers, the API economy is an environment that’s driving industry transformation as a result of cloud, mobile, the Internet of Things and cognitive computing. Application programming interfaces (APIs) are the glue connecting services and systems across and between organizations.For business leaders, when a business delivers an API, it expands its brand and revenue potential. For architects and developers, APIs create efficiencies and fuel innovation, turning a single idea into an industry-disrupting app.Read the white paper, “The Power of the API Economy,” to gain insight into these new business opportunities.
